Our Approach

Using Resources
Wisely

CEO Angela Baltkois built Big Rig Wraps on the principle that sustainability goes beyond recycling — it means using every resource conscientiously and looking for ways to do more with less.

Our business model is inherently eco-friendly: we use advertising space on trucks that are already operating on roads across Canada and North America. No new structures, no additional emissions from our advertising infrastructure.

  • Platinum member of the Green Business Bureau
  • Two-time Sustainability Champion award nominee
  • Reduced paper use by 90% across three locations
  • Extended truck equipment lifetime by ~80 hours annually
  • Minimized plastic, energy, and water consumption company-wide
