Who We Are

More Than a
Wrap Company

Since 2013, Big Rig Wraps has evolved from a vehicle wrap installer into a full-service out-of-home advertising agency with an inclusive, collaborative culture built on shared values.

Our team of brand strategists creates truck advertising campaigns designed to increase visibility and return on investment — while relentlessly tracking performance so you always know your campaign is working.

We believe a diverse team — where every staff member feels valued, respected, and engaged — is the foundation of our competitive edge.

Our Mission

Driving Change on
Every Route

We operate across local, regional, national, and international routes — giving brands of all sizes access to the same high-impact mobile advertising previously reserved for the largest corporations.

Beyond advertising, we're committed to using resources wisely. Our business model places ads on trucks that are already on the road, reducing the environmental footprint of marketing while generating revenue that helps fleet partners invest in cleaner equipment.
