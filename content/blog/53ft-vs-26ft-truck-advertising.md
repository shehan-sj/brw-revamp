One of the first questions every advertiser asks us is simple: **how big should we go?** Here's how to think about it.

## The 26 ft truck — local & regional

26 ft box trucks operate mostly on city streets and local routes, Monday to Friday. They're perfect when your audience is concentrated in a city or region, and they're a lower-cost entry point.

- City streets & local routes
- Lower investment
- Great for local & regional targeting

## The 53 ft trailer — provincial & national

53 ft trailers run **24/7 on highways**, covering provincial and national routes. They offer the largest canvas and the broadest reach — ideal for national brand campaigns.

- Highways & national routes
- Larger surface, higher impact
- Best for national brand awareness

## Which should you pick?

If you're a local business, start with a 26 ft. If you're building national brand awareness, the 53 ft is hard to beat. Not sure? [Request a quote](./quote.html) and we'll recommend the right mix for your goals and budget.
