At Big Rig Wraps, our trucks travel through hundreds of communities across Canada every week — and we believe that comes with a responsibility to honour the people and cultures of the land we drive through.

## Why it matters to us

Diversity and social justice aren't buzzwords for us; they're part of how we've built the company since 2013. Recognizing Indigenous Peoples in Canada is one small way we live those values.

## On the road

From coast to coast, our wrapped trucks pass through traditional territories of First Nations, Inuit, and Métis peoples. We're proud to support awareness campaigns that amplify important messages to millions of Canadians.

> Out-of-home advertising reaches **82% of the population** — which makes it a powerful channel for messages that matter.

If your organization has a message worth spreading, [get in touch](./quote.html) — we'd love to help put it on the highway.
