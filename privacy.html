Privacy Policy

How Big Rig Wraps handles your information and uses analytics on this website.

Big Rig Wraps respects your privacy. This page explains, in plain language, what information this website collects and how it is used.

Information you give us

When you submit a quote request or contact form, we collect the details you provide (such as your name, company, email, phone, and message) solely to respond to your enquiry and prepare a proposal. We do not sell your information, and we do not share it with third parties for their own marketing.

Analytics & cookies

To understand how visitors use the site and to improve it, we use Google Analytics 4. With your consent, it sets cookies that help us measure aggregate, non-identifying information such as:

  • The number of visits and pages viewed
  • Which pages are most popular and how long visitors spend on them
  • The general geographic region visitors come from (country/region)
  • How visitors found us (e.g. search, referral, or an advertising campaign)
  • The type of device and browser used

IP addresses are anonymised. Analytics cookies are only enabled if you choose Accept on the cookie notice; choosing Decline leaves them off. You can change your choice any time by clearing this site's data in your browser, and you can opt out of Google Analytics across all sites using Google's opt-out browser add-on.

Your choices

You can browse the site with analytics declined and still use every feature. To request access to, or deletion of, any information you've submitted through our forms, contact us using the details below.
